About Imua Mana Solutions

Our Story

We Chose Puna. Now We're Helping Power It.

My name is {{ client.about.ownerName }}, and I'm the founder of Imua Mana Solutions LLC. I've spent 28+ years as a licensed Master Electrician and Electrical Contractor — working across industrial grain and chemical facilities, wastewater treatment plants, food packaging operations, commercial buildings, hospitals, and residential projects across the Midwest.

Over the last several years, I shifted my focus to green energy — solar, battery storage, off-grid systems, and the products that make energy independence actually work. I became certified across all the major platforms: Tesla, Enphase, Generac, Sol-Ark, SMA, SolarEdge, Outback. I designed and built systems at residential, commercial, and utility scale.

Then my family and I decided to go further. We tried to go off-grid on the mainland. We ran into the wall that most people run into — strict codes, limited resources, and too few contractors who actually understood what we were trying to do. That experience pushed us toward a different path.

We chose Puna. We purchased land here, built our base of operations, and started building our own systems. And quickly realized: this community needs what we know how to do. Energy independence isn't a lifestyle statement in Puna — it's a real, practical need. Power outages, propane dependence, high utility costs, remote properties. We're here to help solve all of it.

What's Next

We're Building the Future of Big Island Energy

Imua Mana Solutions is more than a service company. Our land in Puna is our home, our office, and our laboratory — and we're actively developing new products designed specifically for Big Island properties.

Residential Thermal Storage System: Once your solar batteries are fully charged, where does the excess power go? Most systems just let it sit. We're developing a system that redirects that excess PV energy into a thermal medium — recovering the heat for water heating, cooking, dehumidification, air cooling, and more. This dramatically reduces dependence on propane and fossil fuels.

Residential-Scale STATCOM / FACTS Device: A second R&D focus is a residential-scale grid stability device. In an off-grid context, this device extends battery runtime and lifetime. On a connected grid, if adopted at scale, the cost savings to power generation utilities are enormous. We're working on making this accessible for individual homes.

The possibilities in Hawai'i are genuinely exciting. We're here to be part of this island's energy future — not just install panels and move on.
