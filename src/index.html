Why Choose Imua Mana

Expertise That Goes Deeper Than Solar

Most solar companies sell you a product. We engineer your energy system — and we live here too. Imua Mana Solutions was built on the Big Island by people who chose Puna because they believe in what's possible here.

  • Industrial-grade engineering knowledge — automation, controls, PLC design, and 28+ years as a licensed master electrician across commercial, industrial, and residential projects.
  • Total transparency — we give you the real facts about your options, help you understand your usage and your goals, and let you choose the solution that fits your life and budget. No pressure.
  • Locally invested innovators — we own land in Puna and are actively developing new energy products for Big Island homes, including residential thermal storage systems for water heating, cooking, and cooling from excess solar.
  • Multi-platform certified — Tesla Powerwall, Enphase, Generac, Sol-Ark, SMA, SolarEdge, and Outback. We know the products and we can help you choose the right one for your situation.
